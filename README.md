# AgroValue_Connect
Support Farmers to Produce Value-Added Agricultural Products

AgroValue Connect is a digital platform designed to empower farmers by enabling them to convert raw agricultural produce into value-added products and connect directly with buyers.

The platform promotes rural entrepreneurship by reducing dependency on middlemen and providing farmers access to broader markets.

🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers often sell raw crops at low prices due to limited market access and lack of value-addition opportunities.
AgroValue Connect addresses this issue by creating a structured marketplace where farmers can showcase processed and handmade agricultural products and sell them directly to buyers.

👥 User Roles
👨‍🌾 Farmer

Add and manage value-added products

Update inventory

Track orders and earnings

🛒 Buyer

Browse and search products

Place orders

Provide ratings and feedback

🔐 Admin

Manage users

Approve product listings

Monitor transactions

Oversee platform activities

✨ Core Features

Role-based dashboards

Product listing and management

Order and transaction workflow

Direct farmer-to-buyer interaction

Clean and user-friendly interface

🌍 Objective

The objective of AgroValue Connect is to:

Increase farmers’ income through value addition

Encourage rural entrepreneurship

Promote digital transformation in agriculture

Create a transparent and efficient marketplace

🏆 Impact

By integrating technology into agriculture, this platform aims to strengthen rural economies and support sustainable development.
